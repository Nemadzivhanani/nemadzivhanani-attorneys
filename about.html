Meet The Director

A legacy of uncompromising advocacy and legal excellence.

Founder & Senior Partner

R. Nemadzivhanani

"Justice is not a privilege reserved for the few; it is a fundamental right that requires aggressive, intelligent defense."

As the founding director of Nemadzivhanani R Inc., Mr. Nemadzivhanani brings extensive expertise and a formidable presence to the High Court of South Africa. With a deep-rooted commitment to protecting the rights of individuals and businesses alike, he has built a practice founded on strategic litigation and meticulous preparation.

Specializing in complex Road Accident Fund (RAF) claims, Deceased Estates, Labour Law, and civil litigation against the State for Unlawful Arrest, he leads a dedicated team of legal professionals committed to yielding maximum results for their clients.

Under his leadership, the firm operates out of dual chambers in the Johannesburg CBD and Roodepoort, ensuring that high-tier legal representation remains accessible to communities across Gauteng.
