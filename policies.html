Legal & Compliance

Our commitment to privacy, client privilege, and ethical practice.

POPIA Compliance

Nemadzivhanani R Inc. is fully compliant with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA). We are committed to protecting our clients' privacy and ensuring that their personal information is used appropriately, transparently, and securely in accordance with applicable laws.

Information collected during consultations, case evaluations, and representation is strictly used for legal purposes and will not be shared with unauthorized third parties without explicit consent.

Client Privilege Policy

Attorney-client privilege is the bedrock of our practice. Any information, documentation, or verbal communication shared between a client and our legal team is strictly confidential.

We maintain rigorous internal security protocols to ensure that your sensitive case details, financial records, and personal statements are protected against disclosure at all times.

Terms & Conditions

By engaging the services of Nemadzivhanani R Inc., clients agree to our standard terms of service. This includes agreements regarding fee structures, billing cycles, and the scope of legal representation.

A formal Letter of Engagement and Mandate detailing these terms will be provided and must be signed before the commencement of any legal proceedings or formal representation.

Website Privacy Policy

This website is managed by Nemadzivhanani IT Solutions. We collect minimal data (such as contact form submissions) solely for the purpose of responding to legal inquiries. We do not use tracking cookies for third-party advertising, nor do we sell user data.
